Spheres

Spheres summarize the physical and mental capabilities of every being in Ordo Universalis. There are three spheres. The first sphere quantifies the core attributes of a creature. General skills are found in the second sphere. Specializations rest in the third and last sphere.

First Sphere

The first sphere is a building block for every creature. Each sphere pulls from its predecessor, and the first sphere precedes them all. Every roll in the game uses the dice determined by the first sphere. There are three physical and three mental attributes that make up this sphere.

  • Constitution = physical cohesiveness
  • Strength = physical muscle
  • Dexterity = physical agility and flexibility
  • Awareness = connection to surroundings
  • Aptitude = ability to process data
  • Fortitude = a force of will

These 1st sphere attributes are closely related to a creature's anatomy. This is why their maximum ratings have a biological limit. The human average for these attributes is 6. However, humans have a biological limit of 14 in each of these 1st sphere attributes. Consider what physical and mental fit you need for the character you want to create.

Each attribute has dice which stems from their respected score. Three is the score at which there are no dice rolled for that attribute. Every other point, after 3, in your attribute will increase your dice size by 2, to a limit of 12. Once you reach a d12 the process repeats, keeping the earlier d12 in the roll. The Attributes and Dice table shows this process.

Second Sphere

The 2nd sphere attributes in Ordo Universalis shows how important the 1st sphere attributes are. Two 1st sphere attributes make one 2nd sphere attribute. These combinations allow for rolls with both 1st sphere attributes die on each related event. The following 2nd sphere attributes make up this sphere.

  • Regimen = Strength + Dexterity
  • = Dexterity + Awareness
  • = Awareness + Aptitude
  • = Aptitude + Fortitude
  • = Fortitude + Constitution
  • = Constitution + Strength

Third Sphere

The last and most specialized sphere is the third sphere. Every 3rd sphere attribute comes from three 1st sphere attributes. These are very precise attributes that hone in on a creature's unique abilities. The following 3rd sphere attributes make up this sphere.

  • Operations = Strength + Dexterity + Awareness
  • Technique = Dexterity + Awareness + Aptitude
  • Mental = Awareness + Aptitude + Fortitude
  • Leadership = Aptitude + Fortitude + Constitution
  • Brute = Fortitude + Constitution + Strength
  • Physical = Constitution + Strength + Dexterity
